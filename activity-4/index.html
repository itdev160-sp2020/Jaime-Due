What's the Deal with Salt Lamps?

Price: $

Student Price: $

Salt lamps, a.k.a. Himalayan Salt Lamps, are rocks of Himalayan pink salt encasing a lightbulb.
Salt lamps illuminate any room with a warm, soft, glow and lay claim to a barrage of benefits including:

  • Air purification
  • Stress relief
  • Sleep assistance
However, whether or not these claims hold any weight is up for debate.
