Manhattan

  • 2 oz whiskey/brandy
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 splashes bitters
  • Suggested Garnish:
    cherries

Old Fashioned

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 splashes bitters
  • 2 oz whiskey/brandy
  • 2 splashes water
  • Suggested Garnish:
    orange peel

Long Island Iced Tea

  • 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz tequila
  • 1/2 oz white rum
  • 1/2 oz gin
  • 1/2 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz sour mix
  • 1 oz cola
  • Suggested Garnish:
    lemon slice

Martini

  • 3 oz gin/vodka
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • Suggested Garnish:
    green olives

Mojito

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • mint leaves
  • club soda
  • Suggested Garnish:
    mint sprig, lime slice
